Brown Sugar Iced Latte

Description

This caffeinated concoction makes for a sweet treat to start the day with. This is an incredibly simple recipe that requires minimal time and ingredients. Note that I prefer to do this with espresso however you can also use a French press or Moka pot to prepare your coffee.

Ingredients

  • Double shot of espresso
  • 1.5 tbsp of brown sugar
  • Handfull of ice
  • 1/4 cup milk of choice

Steps

  1. Combine all your ingredients, minus the milk, in a glass jar. Shake vigorously.
  2. Once your mix is frothy, pour into a drinking glass with a little additional ice and then pour your milk of choice.
  3. Enjoy!
