Chocolate and &aposCaramel&apos Oatmeal

Description

This decadent breakfast is a great start to the day, with complex carbs and a high fiber index. To emulate the caramel flavor, dates are cooked into the oats; resulting in a delicious melt in your mouth experience. Note that the toppings in the picture are merely a serving suggestion and you can use whatever you want.

Ingredients

  • 1/3 cup of oats
  • 1 cup of milk of choice
  • 4 whole dates
  • 1 banana
  • 3tbsp cocoa powder
  • pinch of salt

Steps

  1. Remove any seeds from your dates and chop into small pieces
  2. Cut your banana in half
  3. Heat milk over the stovetop until at a soft simmer
  4. Add in your oats, half of the banana, cocoa powder, salt, and your dates
  5. Make sure to mash the banana into the oats
  6. Cook for 10-15 minutes until it reaches the desired thickness
  7. Slice the remaining banana half into rounds
  8. Serve your oatmeal, topping with the banana slices and any other ingredients you like
