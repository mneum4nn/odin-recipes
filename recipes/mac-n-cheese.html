Mac and Cheese

Description

This three cheesvscode-file://vscode-app/Applications/Visual%20Studio%20Code.app/Contents/Resources/app/out/vs/code/electron-sandbox/workbench/workbench.htmle pasta dish includes cauliflower tossed in a cheesy bechamel along with traditional mac and toasted breadcrumbs on top. The total preparation time ammounts to roughly 3 hours, an hour of which is just waiting time while the casserole bakes. This family favorite is sure to please.

Ingredients

  • 150g Sharp Cheddar
  • 100g Emmental Cheese
  • 150g Gouda
  • 1 head of cauliflower
  • 300g Macaroni pasta
  • 4 tbsp butter
  • 2 tbsp flour
  • Salt
  • 150mL milk
  • 100g Panko breadcrumbs
  1. Wash and chop your cauliflower into florettess
  2. Boil florettes for 6 minutes with salt, then drain any excess water
  3. Start to prepare a bechamel, slowly melting butter in a large saucepan and whisking in flour
  4. Once the roux has emulsified, begin to add in milk slowly while whisking continously to avoid clumping
  5. When your milk butter and flour are homogenous, begin to add in each of your cheeses one at a time, still whihsking. Make sure to set aside some of the cheese (about a handful of each kind)
  6. At this point, begin to boil your pasta and drain when al dente
  7. Preheat your oven to 375 Fahrenheit
  8. Incorporate your macaroni into the cheese sauce
  9. Transfer the mac and cheese into an oven safe dish and top with panko and the cheese you set aside
  10. Bake for 35 minutes and broil for the last 5 until golden
  11. Serve and enjoy!
